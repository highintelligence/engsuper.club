歡迎 Welcome

國際英語學會旨在作為會員與港、澳各英語學術機構等之間溝通的橋樑。
The mission of Internation English Club is to connect members and English institutes in Hong Kong and Macau.

Welcome

宗旨 Missions

  1. 作為會員與港、澳各英語學術機構等之間溝通的橋樑；
  2. 交流英語教與學的經驗及舉辦有關學術講座、研討會等；
  3. 鼓勵會員關注及參與推廣英語的活動，我們分別在香港邀請其他團體參與，務求透過多元化的活動，在香港英語方面達到學術與普及並重的目標。
  1. To connect members and English institutes in Hong Kong and Macau;
  2. To share teaching and learning experiences of English as well as organise related academic talks and seminars, etc.;
  3. To encourage members take part in our joint English activities in Hong Kong, so that the goal of academic English and popularisation can be achieved by these diversified activities.

Activties

合辦課程 Joint Courses

與港、澳各英語學術機構等合辦課程，交流英語教與學的經驗。

Organise joint courses with different English education institutes in both Hong Kong and Macau, to share teaching and learning experiences.

學術講座／研討會 Seminars

舉辦英語學術講座、研討會等，務求透過多元化的活動，在香港英語方面達到學術與普及並重的目標。

Organise English academic talks and seminars, etc., so that the goal of academic English and popularisation can be achieved by these diversified activities.
